About Us

At Melodies For Autism, our mission is to harness the therapeutic power of music to enhance the lives of individuals on the autism spectrum...

Founded by Seyhan Erdeniz, our organization was born out of a deep passion for helping autistic individuals discover their unique talents and abilities through the transformative medium of music.

Programs and Services

  • Music Therapy Sessions
  • Adaptive Music Lessons
  • Music Workshops and Group Classes
  • Music-Inclusive Events
